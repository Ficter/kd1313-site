Kingdom Standards

Simple Rules. Stronger Kingdom.

These are starter rules for a clean Kingdom 1313 page. We can replace them with your exact council rules, MGE rules, KVK rules, and farm policies.

No Unauthorized City Hits

Respect kingdom peace rules unless a player has been marked by leadership.

Follow Event Instructions

For KVK, Ark, MGE, forts, passes, and temples, follow the calls made by assigned leaders.

Respect Resource Zones

Do not tile hit kingdom members. Disputes should go through alliance leadership.

Be Present For KVK

Players are expected to contribute through fighting, reinforcing, gathering, building, or support work.

Keep Chat Clean

No harassment, hate speech, or drama that weakens kingdom unity.

Use Discord For Coordination

Important calls, rally notices, and kingdom updates should be followed in the official Discord.
